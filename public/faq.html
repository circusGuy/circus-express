Frequently Asked Questions

Q:  How much are tickets?
A:  All tickets are $15. Credit card proscessing fees may apply.
(free kids tickets apply to 12 and under)
Q:  How long is the show?
A:  1 hour and 30 minutes
Q:  Can tickets be purchased at the door?
A:  Yes, tickets can be purchased both online and in-person prior to the show. Credit cards and cash both accepted.
Q:  How can I access my tickets after purchasing them online?
A:  Your tickets will be emailed to you. You can print them or save the PDF to your phone for easy access.
Q:  Are there clowns or animals in this show?
A:  No, there are no clowns or animals in this show.
Q:  Are there assigned seats?
A:  No, seating is on a first come first serve basis. All seats have an equal view of the show.
Q:  Can we bring our own food?
A:  No, outside food and drinks are not permitted. Food and drinks are available for purchase before and during the show.
